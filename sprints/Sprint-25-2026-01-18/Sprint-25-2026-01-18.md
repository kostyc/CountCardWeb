# Sprint 25: Page Development & Testing

## Sprint Goal
Develop and test all required pages for the CountCard application based on sprints 1-8, organized in logical order for step-by-step testing and validation.

## Overview
This sprint focuses on creating all user-facing pages identified from sprints 1-8, ensuring each page is functional, accessible, and follows the design system. Pages are organized in logical order for sequential testing and validation.
