## Sprint Goal
Create a comprehensive organizational structure management system for managing Regiments, Battalions, Companies, Series, and Platoons, including hierarchy validation, member management, reporting, analytics, export functionality, and visualization.

## Overview
This sprint focuses on building the complete organizational structure management system for CountCard, providing administrators with tools to manage the complete organizational hierarchy from Regiments down to Platoons, validate organizational relationships, manage members, and generate organizational reports and analytics.
