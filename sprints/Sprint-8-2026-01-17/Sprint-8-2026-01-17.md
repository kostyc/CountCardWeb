## Sprint Goal
Implement comprehensive emergency contact management, extended recruit profile system, profile data encryption, profile data export, profile privacy settings, user profile management including logo management, and the Share Application feature.

## Overview
This sprint focuses on building the emergency contact management system, extended recruit profile features, profile data encryption and export, privacy settings, user profile management with logo sharing capabilities, and the Share Application feature for promoting the application to other drill instructors.

- Sprint 3 security infrastructure must be completed (for encryption)
- Sprint 4 UI/UX foundation must be completed (for UI components)
- Sprint 5 data models and services must be completed (for emergency contacts and profiles)
- Sprint 6 recruit management must be completed (for recruit profiles)
