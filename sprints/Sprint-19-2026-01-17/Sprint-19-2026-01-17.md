## Sprint Goal
Implement comprehensive notifications and alerts system including Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) with VAPID authentication, push notification system, email notification support, notification preferences, alert system for critical events, notification history, notification scheduling, and the Mass Incident Alert System with chain of command workflow and integrated message flow.

## Overview
This sprint focuses on building a complete notifications and alerts system for CountCard, including push notifications, email notifications, in-app notifications, and the comprehensive Mass Incident Alert System with chain of command workflow and integrated messaging capabilities.
